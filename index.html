The Problem

Filipino farmers deserve better

Despite being the backbone of food production, smallholder farmers are trapped in a system that keeps prices low and control out of their hands.

🔗

Middleman dependency

Farmers have no choice but to sell to traders who set unfair prices, especially during calamities when crops risk spoiling.

📉

No price transparency

Without market data, farmers accept whatever offer comes first — often far below what buyers actually pay downstream.

🚚

Logistics burden

Finding transport, coordinating delivery, and managing payments falls entirely on the farmer with no support.

💸

Delayed or unfair payment

Cash-on-delivery arrangements leave farmers vulnerable to non-payment, especially for large orders.
